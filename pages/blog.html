April 16, 2025

Donation Link & Website Live!

By Anvay Ajmera

We're thrilled to announce that our official donation link is now live, and the new Coders Over Borders website is up and running! You can support our mission by donating securely via PayPal. A huge thank-you to everyone who helped beta-test and launch this site—your feedback made all the difference.

April 17, 2025

Branches in South Korea & Ethiopia

By Tharun Naguleswaran

Coders Over Borders is expanding! Today we officially opened new branches in Seoul, South Korea, and Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. These teams will adapt our curriculum for local languages and host workshops to empower students across Asia and Africa. Welcome aboard, new collaborators!

April 18, 2025

Speech at UN ECOSOC

By Anvay Ajmera

Yesterday, I had the honor of delivering a keynote at the United Nations ECOSOC session on youth and digital inclusion. I spoke about how coding education can drive sustainable development and economic opportunity worldwide. Thank you to ECOSOC for the incredible platform—we're ready to turn words into action!
